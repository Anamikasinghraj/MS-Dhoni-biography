Early Life

MS Dhoni was born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Jharkhand. From 2001 to 2003, Dhoni worked as a travelling ticket examiner (TTE) at Kharagpur railway station in West Bengal.

MS Dhoni's Nicknames And Stories

Captain Cool

Dhoni's calm and composed demeanor under pressure earned him this nickname.

Thala

Meaning "leader" in Tamil, this nickname reflects Dhoni's leadership and success in Indian cricket.

Mahi

Given by his friends and family, "Mahi" means "Fish" in Sanskrit, reflecting his love for fishing.

The Finisher

Dhoni's ability to finish matches with his explosive batting earned him this nickname.

Cricket Career

Dhoni made his international debut in 2004 and became one of the most successful Indian captains. He is regarded as one of the best wicket-keepers in the world.

Achievements

  • ICC World Cup winner (2011)
  • ICC Champions Trophy winner (2013)
  • Indian Premier League winner (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023)

Personal Life

Dhoni married Sakshi Singh Rawat in 2010 and has a daughter, Ziva.
